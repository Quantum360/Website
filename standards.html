Datum360 has a long heritage of working with open standards such as ISO 15926, POSC/Caesar, ISO 55000, ISO 14224, BIM, ISA 84, etc.

Datum360's class library platform, CLS360, can map data from our customer's internal standards to industry standards, ensuring compliance with the relevant industry standard.

We are working at the heart of an Information Handover standard for a project called CFIHOS (Capital Facilities Information Handover Specification). It delivers a consistent and explicit specification for the engineering information required for operations across the process and related infrastructure industries.

The Datum360 Connected Data Platform is used to host and manage the CFIHOS Reference Data Library.
